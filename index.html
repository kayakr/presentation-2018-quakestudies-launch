What is Islandora?

  • Digital collection management
  • Institutional repository
  • Research data managemengt
  • (Drupal CMS, Fedora, Solr)

Global community

  • Simon Fraser University
  • University of Sydney
  • University of Rochester
  • University of Prince Edward Island
  • also, NASA...

What was done?

  • Responsive theme, in-page viewers
  • Simplified data model (RDF, linked data)
  • >150,000 objects, 1.3TB, VPN, transcoding, etc.
  • Community code
  • Automated deployment

RDF

  • DC: dc:subject, dc:date, dc:description, etc.
  • DCTERMS: dcterms:spatial, dcterms:temporal
  • schema.org: schema:url
  • DNZ: Usage (hard to derive, so allow direct selection of DigitalNZ options)
  • QSR: Creator Credit - free text
